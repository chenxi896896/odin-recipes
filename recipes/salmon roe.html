Salmon Roe Recipe

Bowl of Salmon Roe

This fragrant bowl of delicious salmon roe is to die for.

Ingredients

  • Female salmon
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Give female salmon a good hard squeeze.
  2. Give eggs a good wash until clean.
  3. Add salt as desired.
  4. Package and sell for an excessive amount of money.
